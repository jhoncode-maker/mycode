ALEJOS FALLS

Alejos Falls is situated in the heart of Brgy. Dao, Alejos. The falls highlights a capacious pool with crystal-clear turquoise water cascading freely. This falls serves as the municipalities reservoir, featuring a solace scenery that nestled in the midst of tress and profuse vegetation, offering shade and a serene environment for bathers and swimmers alike.

FLOATING COTTAGES

This famous site is located at Barangay Poblacion near the town's center. The 3 Floating cottages offer activities like swimming, sliding, and picnic with a great view of Bontoc port and boulevard.

ANAHAO CAVE

These are the two of the known caves in the municipality of Bontoc. It has live stalactites and stalagmites, and roost for different species of bats. ANAHAO CAVE and CATMON CAVE are popularly known for their rich source of "guano" from the accumulated exrement and remains of birds and bats. It is a 15 to 35 minute nature walk from the Barangay site to main cave.

MT. PANGI

Mt. Pangi is located at Brgy. Pangi, Bontoc, Southern Leyte. This grandeur site offers unparalleled landscape, grandiose view and breathtaking sight. This tourist spot is not only ideal for hiking enthusiasts, but to everyone who enjoys green sceneries and adventurous trekking.

PAMIGSIAN TRINITY FALLS

PAMIGSIAN TRINITY FALLS Botong, Pag-amuan and Tag-abaka Falls are considered the Trinity Falls of Bontoc. The waterfalls are set in a natural environment rich in green foliage with crystal-clear cold-water spring. The Trinity Falls are found in the heart of the forest in Brgy. Pamigsian and boast of an adventure worth taking. The site is perfect for trekking, hiking, and swimming. One can reach the Trinity Falls after about one and a half hours hike from the barangay center.
