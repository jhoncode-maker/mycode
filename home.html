Bontoc Shine Bright

In a resounding triumph for the Municipality of Bontoc, the indomitable leadership of Mayor Noel E. Alinsub and the unwavering guidance of MLGOO Ms. Glenda Alipao have propelled the town to new heights.

The Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG) Region 8 recently bestowed upon Bontoc the prestigious title of one of the Best Performing Local Government Units in Eastern Visayas during the Regional SubayBAYANI Awards Ceremony held on December 19, 2023, at The Oriental in Leyte, Palo.
